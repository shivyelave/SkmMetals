SKM Group commenced its operations in the year 1969 under the guidance and leadership of the founder Mr. Kirti Shah, a visionary who saw potential in the distribution of ferrous and non-ferrous metals. Over the years, his perseverance paid off and the group has diversified into various business activities which have helped the industry grow as a whole. Today, SKM Steel Limited has its own Production & Processing Facilities and Service Centres in India which are functional round the clock and produce the finest quality of stainless steel products and are renowned for servicing mild steel.
